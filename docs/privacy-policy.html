Privacy Policy

RootADB Pro | Last updated: January 3, 2025

Summary: RootADB Pro does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All app data is stored locally on your device only.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

RootADB Pro operates entirely on your device and does not:

  • Collect personal data
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, photos, or files
  • Send any data to external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking services

Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences:

  • Settings: ADB port, boot preferences, theme selection
  • Trusted Devices: IP addresses of devices you've approved for ADB connections
  • Warpgate Configuration: SSH tunnel settings (if configured)

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Permissions Used

The app requires certain permissions to function:

  • INTERNET: Required for ADB connections over network
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To detect your device's IP address
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: To enable ADB automatically on device boot (optional)
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: To keep the ADB relay service running
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To show connection status in the notification bar

Third-Party Services

RootADB Pro does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics, or advertising platforms.

If you use optional features like Tailscale or Warpgate, those connections are made directly to servers you configure. We have no access to these connections.

P2P Token System

The P2P Token feature generates temporary connection tokens locally on your device. These tokens:

  • Are generated entirely on-device
  • Expire after 30 minutes by default
  • Are never transmitted to any server
  • Are shared only if you manually share them with another user

Children's Privacy

This app is a developer tool and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
